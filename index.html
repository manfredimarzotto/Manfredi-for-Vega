30 / 60 / 90-Day Plan

How I'd ramp — mixing process execution, special projects, and compounding systems.

Day 30 Learn & Scope
  • Map stakeholder priorities across CEO Office, Product, Engineering, and GTM; agree on success metrics + cadence.
  • Identify 1–2 high-leverage special projects and define scope, owners, milestones, and decision logs.
  • Draft a lightweight investor reporting rhythm: narrative template + KPI list + timeline.
Day 60 Ship & Operationalize
  • Run 1 special project end-to-end and ship the first version; collect feedback and iterate fast.
  • Operationalize cross-team execution: weekly operating review, unblock list, and clear escalation paths.
  • Stand up repeatable investor update materials that reduce thrash and increase decision velocity.
Day 90 Scale & Compound
  • Scale the special-project playbook (intake → prioritization → delivery) so it's not person-dependent.
  • Support geographic expansion planning (e.g., New York): roles, timeline, dependencies, and execution plan.
  • Deliver a coherent "Vega story" across product progress + GTM learnings + operating cadence.
